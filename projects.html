Our Projects

Social Housing Project aims to provide affordable housing to low-income communities, helping to improve living standards and create vibrant communities. It includes the construction of new homes and the rehabilitation of existing structures.

Usizo Labantu is a social initiative focused on uplifting disadvantaged communities through skills development, education, and access to basic services. We aim to empower individuals and help break the cycle of poverty.

The Vodacom Project is a technology-driven initiative to improve telecommunications and internet accessibility in remote and underserved regions. The project provides mobile network coverage and internet connectivity to rural areas.

Fuel On is an energy sustainability project focused on providing affordable and eco-friendly fuel solutions to local communities. The project aims to promote renewable energy sources, reduce carbon footprints, and improve access to energy in rural areas.
