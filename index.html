Nhlanhla Goodman Makhanya, a prominent radio presenter at Vuma FM, has become a well-known voice in the South African media industry. His passion for radio and engaging storytelling has made him a favorite among listeners. Beyond his work as a radio presenter, Nhlanhla is an accomplished author, having written a book titled *It's All About The Money*, which offers invaluable insights on personal finance, business strategies, and wealth creation. He is also a seasoned business consultant, providing expertise to various organizations on improving their financial management and business operations. In addition to his media and consulting work, Nhlanhla is a motivational speaker, inspiring others with his journey of financial success and personal development. His diverse career spans multiple industries, showcasing his dedication to empowering others through his knowledge and experiences in business, finance, and media.
